Agentic AIoT LLM Dashboard & Mobile App

2025

AIoT platform that connects an AGV and factory sensors to a web dashboard and mobile app for multi-modal monitoring, analysis, and predictive maintenance. Current focus: acoustics-based defect detection on industrial components.

  • Unified telemetry from the AGV (position, battery, motor current) and sensors (acoustic, vibration, temperature) into one stream.
  • Acoustics pipeline: streaming capture → feature extraction (spectral / MFCC-style) → anomaly scoring & defect classification.
  • Live website shows device state, sensor charts, events, and logs; mobile client syncs via WebSocket.
  • Agentic LLM workflows triage alerts and suggest checks/spare parts; device actions exposed via the MCP protocol.

Lexo Leg Exoskeleton

2024

Semi-active lower-limb exoskeleton designed for older adults. The user initiates the step and the system adds timed assistance to reduce effort while preserving a natural gait (walking / light running).

  • User-in-the-loop assist: provides a power boost at the right phase of the step instead of fully powered motion.
  • Senior-friendly design: adjustable fit, comfortable strapping, simple don/doff, and focus on stability.
  • Iteration (V1 → V4): addressed frame stiffness/weight, knee joint alignment, fit/comfort, and assist smoothness across four versions.
  • Outcome: more efficient steps with reduced perceived effort while keeping user control.

Green Bio Blocks

2023

Eco-friendly brick concept turning wastepaper into useful material, with automation for consistency, lower cost, and scalability.

  • Efficient waste management: converts readily available wastepaper into pulp, reducing landfill load.
  • Cleaner materials: substitutes part of cement with paper-based sludge to reduce dust/health concerns.
  • Automation: paper collection → shredding → precise mixing → molding → curing; less labor, 24/7 potential.
  • Consistent quality: precise ratios and real-time monitoring raise uniformity toward industry standards.
  • Cost-effective: budget-friendly automation vs heavy machinery lowers per-block cost.
  • Customizable & scalable: swappable molds and programmable recipes; increase throughput by adding molds or tank size.
